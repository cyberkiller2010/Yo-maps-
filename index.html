About

Elton Mulenga (born 1994), known as Yo Maps, is a Zambian singer, songwriter, and producer. He rose to fame with the 2018 hit "Finally" featuring Macky 2.

His album Komando (2021) racked up 18 million streams in its first month. He made history as the first artist to sell out Heroes Stadium in Lusaka, drawing 40,000+ fans.

"My mission is to put Zambian music on the global map."
